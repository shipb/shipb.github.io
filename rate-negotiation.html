Four Steps. Full Transparency.

From invoice upload to savings proof in minutes.

1

Upload the Invoice

Your client uploads the customer's current carrier invoice into ShipBoss. This invoice is the ground truth for every line item.

2

Re-Quote Line by Line

ShipBoss takes each shipment from the invoice and re-quotes it to determine what would have been paid under the old rates. If any data is missing, we fall back to the current rate card.

3

Compare Against Old Rates

We compare the current invoice against the previously negotiated rates stored in the system. ShipBoss rebuilds the rate difference so you see the exact savings per shipment.

4

Invoice the Customer

With the savings calculated, generate a branded invoice showing the customer exactly what you saved them — and what they owe for your services.
