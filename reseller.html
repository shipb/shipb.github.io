A Powerful Platform for Growth

Multi-Carrier Integration

Simplify Your Shipping. Unlock the power of choice. Seamlessly manage FedEx, UPS, DHL, and Stamps.com shipments from a single dashboard. Features like Google API address search and our rate quote carousel save time and ensure you always choose the most cost-effective option.

E-commerce Simplified

Direct Platform Integration. Automate your order fulfillment with direct integrations into Magento, Shopify, and WooCommerce. ShipBoss pulls your orders in real-time, allowing you to focus on growing your business instead of manual entry.

Comprehensive Customer Management

Unlimited Flexibility. Manage your customers with unparalleled ease. Set up unlimited users, manage contacts and billing addresses, and input discount rates all in one place. Our "sign-in as user" feature lets you provide targeted support and ensure their success.

Tailor Your Shipping Experience

Custom and Default Settings. Control how you ship. Customize package details, references, and label types to fit unique needs, or set default addresses and preferences for quicker processing. Our platform adapts to your business for perfect balance and efficiency.

Advanced Invoice Management

Maximize Your Margins. Revolutionize your billing. Upload carrier invoices, and let our platform match shipments, calculate discounts, and generate billing. With detailed profit reports, you'll always know where your business stands.

Unlock Hidden Profits with The Optimizer

Stop leaving money on the table. Our intelligent Optimizer automatically analyzes all your available carrier accounts and rates for every single shipment. It instantly identifies the most profitable option, often uncovering savings you didn't know existed. It's a no-risk way to boost your bottom line. We're so confident, our fee is simply 30% of the extra profit we find for you. If you don't make more, we don't get paid.

Prove Your Value with Rate Negotiation Analytics

Renegotiated a client's UPS contract? Upload the old and new rate cards and ShipBoss generates a full comparison — service by service, zone by zone. Then benchmark against FedEx and DHL. Generate branded savings reports that justify your fee and keep clients long-term.
