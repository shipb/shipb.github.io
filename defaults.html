Personalize Your Shipping Process 
With ShipBoss's Customizable Shipping Defaults feature, you have the power to tailor your shipping process according to your preferences and operational needs.  Benefits of Setting Your Shipping Defaults

Increased Efficiency:

 Spend less time on setup by applying your preferred shipping options automatically to new shipments. 

Personalized Shipping Experience:

Tailor the shipping process to match your specific requirements and preferences, ensuring a consistent approach that fits your business model. 

Time Savings:

Reduce the need for repetitive manual inputs, allowing you to focus more on your business growth and less on logistics.
