 Simplify Your Shipping in Four Easy Steps 

Step 1

Sign Up

Get started by signing up for ShipBoss. It's quick, easy, and unlocks the full suite of shipping management tools.

Step 2

Integrate Your E-commerce Platform

Connect ShipBoss with your e-commerce platform (Magento, Shopify, WooCommerce, with more platforms being added) to automatically import your orders. This seamless integration saves you time and reduces manual entry.

Step 3

Create and Manage Labels

Use ShipBoss to effortlessly generate shipping labels for your orders. Our platform allows you to create, manage, and reprint labels from a range of carriers, ensuring you always get the best rates.

Step 4

Track Your Shipments

Keep an eye on your shipments with our real-time tracking feature. ShipBoss updates you on the status of each shipment, providing peace of mind and transparency to you and your customers.
